Expertise

Protection & Testing Capability

A broad technical capability covering secondary injection, primary injection, scheme verification, numerical relay platforms, interlocks, differential and distance schemes, and disturbance analysis.

Core Skill Set

Testing and commissioning of protection relays, current transformers, capacitive voltage transformers, circuit breakers, isolators, earth switches, and lightning arresters.

Scheme checking for control and relay panels, switchyard and switchgear interlocks, and DG auto change-over schemes.

Testing of numerical protection relays for distance, transformer differential, generator protection, motor protection, line differential, overcurrent, earth fault, voltage, and frequency protections.

Busbar and transformer stability and sensitivity testing using primary and secondary injection for single bus, transfer bus, double bus, and one-and-a-half breaker schemes.

Relay-to-relay communication setup using GOOSE for carrier communication, direct transfer schemes, and interlocks.

Relay setting coordination knowledge for distance, transformer, REF, overcurrent, and earth fault protections.

Disturbance record analysis including waveform studies, sequence and time period component calculation, harmonic calculation, and ranging functions.
